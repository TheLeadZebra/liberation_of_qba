The Temple of [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Astilabor\|Astilabor]] features a floor mural of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Astilabors Peak/Points of Interest/Astilabor's Peak Mountain\|Astilabor's Peak Mountain]], and a large statue of [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Astilabor\|Astilabor]] stands at the far end of the chapel: A topaz dragon, shimmering with a purple iridescence and an underbelly of gold.

This temple retains the grandeur often found among Temples to [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Astilabor\|Astilabor]], it is expected to be filled with lavish furnishings, and expensive treasures, such as platinum candle holders and sconces. Each treasure within the temple was always marked clearly with who the owner was, often marked using particular characteristics defining an individual, so as to prove the wealth still belonged to them.

The temple also features a plentiful amount of bookcases filled with books of knowledge, each also featuring a unique marking or stamp defining who each belongs to. 

The temple was ransacked in recent times by humans, claiming that the draconic god encouraged greed and hoarding of wealth.
