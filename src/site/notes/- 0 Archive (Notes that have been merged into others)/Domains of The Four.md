Each of [[The World/Deities/The Four Cardinal Gods\|The Four Cardinal Gods]] have divided the 8 standard Clerical domains between them, each residing over 2. 

More information about the control of domains by each of the [[The World/Deities/Deities\|Deities]] can be found within their own article within [[The World/Deities/The Four Cardinal Gods\|The Four Cardinal Gods]].

#### Domains by Deity
- Death - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Null\|Null]] - The Death domain is concerned with the forces that cause death, as well as the negative energy that gives rise to undead creatures.
- Knowledge - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Astilabor\|Astilabor]] - The domain of Knowledge values learning and understanding above all. [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Astilabor\|Astilabor]] teaches that knowledge is to be gathered and hoarded, only to be shared with friends or allies.
- Life - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Tamara\|Tamara]] - The Life domain focuses on the vibrant positive energy which sustains all life. the God of Life promotes vitality and health through healing the sick and wounded, caring from those in need, and driving away the forces of death and undeath. [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Tamara\|Tamara]] too values the draconic element of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Races/Dragonkin/Dragonborn of Q'ba\|Poison]], with an understanding that all which heals may also kill in the incorrect dosage.
- Light - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Garyx\|Garyx]] - The God of Light promotes the ideals of rebirth and renewal, truth, vigilance, and beauty. 
- Nature - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Tamara\|Tamara]] - Gods of nature are varied, but [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Tamara\|Tamara]] presents herself as a kind God of the forest, who inspires her followers to keep [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Dimmafall/Dimmafall\|Dimmafall]] free of corrpution and monstrosities.
- Tempest - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Garyx\|Garyx]] - As a God of Tempest, [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Garyx\|Garyx]] presents as a god of fire, and of thunder and lightning. Known for swift justice applied to any who cross him, and his Clerics inspire folk, with displays of strength, to remain on the path of righteousness.
- War - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Null\|Null]] - Despite it's negative conotations, War can have many manifestations. It can make heroes of the ordinary, it may be desperate, horrific and cruel, or cowardly. It may also exist as a demonstration of justice, vengeance, excellence or courage. As the God of War, Null watches over great warriors and reward their deeds.
- Trickery - [[- 0 Archive (Notes that have been merged into others)/Astilabor\|Astilabor]] - Gods of Trickery are michief-makers and instigators who stand as a constant challenge to the accepted order among both gods and mortals. They are patrons of thieves, scoundrels, gamblers, rebels and liberators, and their clerics are a disruptive force which punctures pride, mock tyrants, steal from the rich and free captives. Those followers of Trickery prefer subterfuge, pranks and deception to direct confrontation.
