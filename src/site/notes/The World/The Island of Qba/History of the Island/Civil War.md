300 years ago the city of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Drakenholm/Drakenholm\|Drakenholm]] was ensnared by a Civil War between [[Story/Factions/Nobles\|Nobles]] and [[Story/Factions/Commoners\|Commoners]]. It is understood that they disagreed over who should have become [[The World/The Island of Qba/History of the Island/The Rule of Qba/The Crown\|The Crown]]. These individuals are now known only by their names within the legend; [[The World/People of the World/Folk Tales/The Just\|The Just]] and [[The World/People of the World/Folk Tales/The Greedy\|The Greedy]]. Much of the city was destroyed and has since been rebuilt. Only the [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Drakenholm/Drakenholm#Old Town\|Old Town]] and [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Drakenholm/Drakenholm#The Castle\|The Castle]] and surrounding remain mostly in their original forms.

It is believed that after this civil war the island was blessed with the interests of [[The World/Deities#Bahamut\|Bahamut]] and [[The World/Deities#Tiamat\|Tiamat]], introducing [[The World/Deities#The Binary Gods\|The Binary Gods]] in addition to the already established [[The World/Deities#The Four Cardinal Gods\|The Four Cardinal Gods]] of the island.
