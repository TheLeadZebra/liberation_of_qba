The mountain of Astilabor's Peak rises high over all else on the island of [[The World/The Island of Qba/The Island of Qba\|The Island of Qba]], dwarfing even [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Drakenholm/Districts of Drakenholm/The Castle\|The Castle]] in the city of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Drakenholm/Drakenholm\|Drakenholm]]. This mountain is found within the island's region of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Astilabors Peak/Astilabors Peak\|Astilabors Peak]], attuned with the element of cold, and the domain of the [[The World/Deities\|Deities]] [[The World/Deities#Astilabor\|Astilabor]]. 

The mountain and it's surrounding landscape are covered with a layer of snow and ice all year round, and it is known that the area is abound with precious gems and metals which are mined by [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Races/Non-Human/Dwarves of Q'ba\|Dwarves]] and [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Races/Non-Human/Goblins of Q'ba\|Goblins]]. 

At the foot of the southern face of the mountain is the village of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Astilabors Peak/Settlements/Astilabor's Claw\|Astilabor's Claw]], as well as the mine known as [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Astilabors Peak/Points of Interest/Astilabor's Tail\|Astilabor's Tail]].
