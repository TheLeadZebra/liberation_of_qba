The Statue of Greenscale is a Great Work of Art produced by [[The World/People of the World/Artisans/Stor Ondun\|Stor Ondun]].

The statue is 8 foot tall and sits on a plinth 2 foot tall. The statue was created using [[The World/Regional Specialisms/Q'ban Anodised Titanium\|Q'ban Anodised Titanium]] and depicts [[The World/People of the World/Rulers/Draumur 'Greenscale' Funahjarta\|Draumur 'Greenscale' Funahjarta]] as [[The World/The Island of Qba/History of the Island/The Rule of Qba/The Crown\|The Crown]]. It was crafted to celebrate his reign, and in memory of the destruction of the [[The World/The Island of Qba/History of the Island/History of the Island#Land Ho\|Pirate Attack on Q'ba]].
