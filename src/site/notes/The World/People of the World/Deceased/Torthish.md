Torthish was a Noble dragonborn who joined [[The World/Regional Authorities/Whole Island/The Crown Guard/The Crown Guard\|The Crown Guard]] as a [[The World/Regional Authorities/Whole Island/The Crown Guard/Melee/Duellist\|Duellist]]. He had an old, school rivalry with [[The World/People of the World/Allies/Q'ba/Draxis Bhann\|Draxis Bhann]].

According to [[The World/People of the World/Allies/Q'ba/Buster DeWhitt\|Buster DeWhitt]] he found that he has the opportunity to fight [[The World/People of the World/Allies/Q'ba/Draxis Bhann\|Draxis Bhann]] and gladly took it. Over confident in his abilities his attack was parried away by [[The World/People of the World/Allies/Q'ba/Draxis Bhann\|Draxis Bhann]], who swiftly ended the conflict.
