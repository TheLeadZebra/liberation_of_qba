The Night Guide is a [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Races/Dragonkin/Dragonborn of Q'ba\|White Dragonborn]] who roams the streets of [[The World/The Island of Qba/Island Regions/Drakenholm\|Drakenholm]] offering protection to any who are worried to walk in the dark. 

He is known to carry a [[Moon-Touched Sword\|Moon-Touched Glaive]] and wear thick, heavy robes.
